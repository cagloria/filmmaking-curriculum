Set your high schoolers to master the art of visual storytelling through our filmmaking curriculum.

The future of film

Magenta Origins holds the key to ensuring the success of your high school's future filmmakers. Our curriculum holds courses in writing, video production, film history, design, special effects, marketing, and much more.

In-person

Real and tangible, students will get training in equipment and software, putting what they learn from classroom lectures to practice.

Remote

Top-tier digital courses prepare students to apply their knowledge with provided equipment and software sent to your school or their homes.

Hybrid

A mix of both worlds, students will apply what they learn in online courses to in-person labs and practice classes.
